## Abstract

We explain as a case study format our experiences when integrating ChatGPT-styled tools inside our workflows of the Open Innovation team. To further illustrate our point, we engage the audience by working through two activities regarding _prompting_ and _data preparation_ to facilitate our job with these tools. The outline progression explains why these AI tools facilitate the understanding, learning, and integration phases of two particular Tizen Native APIs (_`audio_io.h` and `message_port.h`_), which lack any cohesive practical and modernized examples. All topics are explored by emphasizing an ethically responsible usage of the tools, while considering possible cognitive biases and limitations inside our own internal knowledge base.
